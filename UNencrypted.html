GREEN FLAG ACTIONS

1. RESEARCH WORK

Once you get into this, you will feel good.
  • Music on Spotify

  • Work at your desk!

  • Make a drink and a snack.

  • Post on Slack for personal accountability.

2. HUBBLE

Hubble is your most important little guy.
  • Hot girl walk.

  • 5 minute timer for play.

  • Training (shaping?).

  • T-touch massage.

  • Make treats together.

3. MOVEMENT

Pick an activity quickly, without wasting time! The first one is a good one.
  • Joyn on YouTube.

  • Yoga with Adrienne on YouTube.

  • Down Dog HIIT.

  • 10 min. biking with TV.

4. CARETASKS

Half done is better than not done!
  • Put on some music.

  • Agree to work for just 5 min.

  • "Step down" to the harder tasks.

  • Warm body is last resort after a substatial effort was made.


5. MELTDOWN (or OVERWHELM) MANAGEMENT

STOP: Stop, take step back, observe, proceed mindfully.
  • Use distraction to reset: puzzle, TV, Hubble, etc.

  • It's okay if you have to throw away / remove things.

  • Communicate to others so they understand you are not being rude.


6. CONTACT NUMBERS

You can call a number here for support. But this is for targeted support, not just because you are lonely.
